## App Store Description

### Short Description (First paragraph - visible without "Read More")

Track your daily steps, build unbreakable walking streaks, and boost your fitness with science-backed interval training. Just Walk protects your progress with StreakGuard technology and helps you walk smarter - not just more.

### Full Description

**BUILD A WALKING HABIT THAT STICKS**

Just Walk makes it easy to hit 10,000 steps a day and build a lasting walking habit. Track your progress with beautiful widgets, protect your streaks from missed days, and level up with interval training when you're ready.

**JUST WALK (Free)**
- Track unlimited walks with GPS routes
- Set goals by time, distance, or steps
- Beautiful step ring shows daily progress
- 7-day activity history
- Home screen and lock screen widgets
- Full Apple Watch app with complications

**POWER WALK (Pro)**
Science-backed interval training that burns 20% more calories in less time:
- 3-minute easy / 3-minute brisk intervals
- Audio and haptic pace cues
- Choose Quick (18 min), Standard (30 min), or Extended (45 min)

**NEVER LOSE YOUR STREAK AGAIN**
- StreakGuard rescues streaks from data sync failures
- Streak Shields let you "pardon" occasional missed days (3/month with Pro)
- See your streak chain with a beautiful 45-day calendar view

**21 ACHIEVEMENTS TO UNLOCK**
Progress through badges across three categories:
- Consistency achievements for daily streaks
- Distance milestones for total miles walked
- Step count goals for lifetime steps

**WORKS ON YOUR WRIST**
Full standalone Apple Watch app syncs via iCloud:
- Start walks directly from your watch
- Complications for watch face
- No iPhone required during walks

**PRO MEMBERSHIP ($39.99/year)**
- Power Walk interval training
- 3 Streak Shields per month
- Route tracking & saved routes
- Extended history & advanced insights
