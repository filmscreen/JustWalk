## Key Messages (Copy-Paste Ready)

### Tagline
> Walk Smarter. See Results.

### One-liner
> Just Walk helps you build lasting walking habits with streak protection and interval training.

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
> Just Walk is for people who've tried step counters but gave up when they lost a streak to a sync error or missed one day. We protect your progress with StreakGuard technology and help you walk smarter with interval training - all from your wrist.

### Feature Bullets
- StreakGuard rescues streaks from sync failures
- Streak Shields let you "pardon" occasional missed days
- Power Walk interval training burns 20% more calories
- Full standalone Apple Watch app
- 21 progressive achievements to unlock

## Target Audience Summary

**Primary:** Health-conscious adults (30-55) who want to walk more consistently

**Segments:**
1. **Streak Chasers** - Motivated by streaks, hate losing progress
2. **Efficiency Seekers** - Want results in limited time (Power Walk)
3. **Gadget Lovers** - Apple Watch owners wanting polished apps
4. **Habit Builders** - New to fitness, need encouragement
